About Me



I'm a Digital Artist based in the Philippines, I've been doing digital art ever since I got my first art tablet at 15. I've already began working with clients from around the world at just 17 gaining tons of work experience at a young age. I'm a versatile artist able to adopt different types of artworks and artstyles such as backgrounds, character design, and concept art.

Now at the age of 20 I'm currently taking my Bachelors Degree in Multimedia Arts at the University of the Cordilleras. I'm continuing to grow my skills in art and exploring different types of art style to further my knowledge about the medium.
